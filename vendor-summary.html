01
Innostar Bio
Shanghai, China · Preclinical CRO
Small Molecules DMPK Tox FDA-GLP
+

A premier preclinical and clinical-stage CRO offering end-to-end drug development services from discovery to regulatory submission. Founded in 2010, supports small molecules, biologics, and cell therapies. GLP-certified by US FDA, OECD (Netherlands), and China's NMPA.

Has evaluated nearly 750 compounds including over 500 small molecules across 140+ global clients. Services include nonclinical toxicology, DMPK, bioanalysis, biomarker analysis, and IND submission support.

Best for: Preclinical safety and PK studies with multi-regulatory acceptance (FDA, EMA, CFDA).
02
WuXi AppTec
Global · Integrated CRO / CRDMO
DMPK Safety Assessment Bioanalytical Full Pipeline
+

Their Laboratory Testing Division is a comprehensive integrated testing platform supporting customers across the full spectrum of development — from early drug discovery through to market. Services span DMPK, safety assessment, and bioanalytical testing.

One of the largest and most full-service CRO/CDMO platforms globally, with a drug discovery engine and manufacturing capabilities in addition to testing.

Best for: When you need one partner to handle testing across multiple modalities and stages with strong global regulatory standing.
03
Bora Pharmaceuticals
US · Canada · Taiwan · Drug Product CDMO
Drug Product Fill/Finish cGMP Mfg Sterile Injectable
+

A premier international CDMO specializing in formulation development, clinical and commercial manufacturing, and packaging of complex oral solid dose, liquid, semi-solid, biologics, and sterile injectable products. Delivers to more than 100 markets worldwide.

Baltimore facility has extensive experience in tech transfer, scale-up, and commercial production of both small molecules and biological compounds. 9 cGMP facilities across North America and Taiwan, approved by FDA, EMA, Health Canada, and others.

Best for: Drug product manufacturing — once your molecule is ready for formulation, clinical supply, or commercial scale.
04
GemPharmatech
Nanjing, China · San Diego, US · In Vivo CRO
Mouse Models In Vivo Efficacy Humanized Mice Oncology
+

Leading provider of genetically engineered mouse models (GEMMs) and preclinical research services globally. Maintains a collection of 25,000+ mouse strains including KO/cKO, humanized, immunodeficient, and germ-free mice — one of the world's largest libraries.

San Diego expansion (2025) provides local lab space, mouse breeding, and in vivo preclinical studies. Assay platforms include PK/PD, flow cytometry, histopathology, behavioral analysis, and in vivo imaging.

Best for: In vivo mouse efficacy work — oncology/immunology, metabolic disease, autoimmune models. Local US access now available in San Diego.
05
Kyinno Biotechnology
Beijing · Boston (Waltham, MA) · Oncology CRO
CRISPR Cell Lines Small Molecules PROTACs In Vitro Screening
+

A preclinical CRO focused on oncology drug discovery with 8,000+ engineered cell lines and specialized CRISPR knock-in/knock-out capabilities. Provides in vitro and in vivo pharmacology, cell line engineering, therapeutic target identification, and antibody discovery.

Services include CRISPR cell line construction, cell-based assay validation, drug screening for small molecules and PROTACs, FACS assays, ADCC/ADCP bioassays, and PK/PD efficacy studies in immunodeficient mouse models. Over 700 BaF3 kinase cell lines for kinase inhibitor screening.

Best for: CRISPR-based testing, small molecule / PROTAC target engagement assays, and cell-based pharmacology screening.
06
Porton Pharma Solutions
China · US · EU · Small Molecule CDMO
API Manufacturing CMC Process Dev PROTACs
+

A global small molecule CDMO with end-to-end API development and manufacturing from preclinical to commercial production. Services include process design, route scouting, GMP manufacturing, solid form selection (polymorphs, salts, cocrystals), and IND/NDA dossier support.

Over 4,200 employees, 18 sites across US, EU, and China. Received the "Global Small Molecule API Award" at the 2025 Outsourced Pharma CDMO Leadership Awards. Strong capabilities in PROTACs, continuous chemistry, biocatalysis, and HPAPI.

Best for: Small molecule API chemistry, CMC development, and process scale-up from pre-clinical through commercial manufacturing.
07
BioLegacy Research
San Diego, CA · Full-Service Preclinical CRO
GLP / Non-GLP In Vivo / In Vitro IND-Enabling Local SD
+

San Diego-based CRO delivering GLP and non-GLP biological services since 2001 (originally Bio-Quant). Expertise in in vitro and in vivo research across inflammation/autoimmunity, oncology, and metabolic disorders, testing biologics, small molecules, natural products, and medical devices.

Guides pipelines from early discovery to IND submission with on-site bioanalytical capabilities, multi-species models (mice through NHPs), GLP toxicology, and safety pharmacology. Agile and flexible with fast study startup.

Best for: Local San Diego partner for early-stage discovery through IND-enabling studies. Strong for biotech startups needing flexibility and direct scientist access.
